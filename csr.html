Corporate Social Responsibility

Advent Global is a firm believer in responsible sourcing and has consciously aligned it to our internal processes and practices. Our roadmap and sourcing actions are drawn upon our commitment and integrated approach to corporate social responsibility. We have taken initiatives in some areas that align with our regular business practices to ensure that we are making a positive contribution to the society and also adhering to our clients’ responsible sourcing policies:

  • Environmental Management
  • Workforce Welfare and Development
  • Ethical and Social Responsibility
  • Continuous Monitoring and Improvement

We comply with laws related to our activities and work with our clients and staff to ensure that we continually reduce our impacts on people and our environment. Our commitment to sustainability is guided by our corporate principles that drive our goal setting, management, and documentation of progress in all facets of sustainability – social, environmental, and economic.
