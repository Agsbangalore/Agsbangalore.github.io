Our Business Model

Our business model is very unique in the contingent workforce arena – unlike most of our competitors who have a geographical go-to market strategy doing business with multiple companies in a specific geography, Advent Global is vertically-aligned and is a very client-centric organization.

We conduct our business with select clientele that are industry leaders in their specific verticals, providing them with low-overhead and high-volume oriented recruiting services. Our business model is based on our metric-driven approach that is specifically designed to align itself to the current prevailing MSP/ VMS environment. This further complements our client MSP programs and results in pre-calculated performance for our clients.

We currently engage with over mid-to-large (including Fortune 500) MSP/VMS run client CW programs and our exceptional performance across these programs gives us the confidence to support other similar client engagements in the most effective manner.

About Us

Headquartered in Houston (Texas), Advent Global is a nationally certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) providing IT/Non-IT Contingent Workforce Services across 49 states in United States. Established in 1997, Advent Global has strong recruiting capabilities to identify the best fit candidates for a variety of requirements across diverse domains and categories.
