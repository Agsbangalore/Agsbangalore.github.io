Enterprise Mobility

Advent Global offers a full suite of mobile enterprise solutions comprising of Strategy Building, Design, Custom Development, Testing and Deployment. Our enterprise mobility solutions address specific functionality needs of today’s mobile workforce thus empowering companies with apps ranging from field sales, field service, field maintenance and repair, logistics, transportation and others. Leveraging our proprietary Mobile Channel Access Gateway (MAG), our applications can integrate universally to any back-end ERP (Oracle, SAP. Microsoft) or front-end mobile platform (Android, Apple, Microsoft). Our flexible architecture allows us to provide scalable and inter-operable “Build once deploy many” universal set of mobile business applications for our clients.

We also bring to you a seasoned team of 100+ industry experts and enterprise mobility visionaries with a combined experience of 1000+ person-years of delivering mobility value to enterprises. Our depth of experience in enterprise mobility spans across diverse industries, proven platforms, processes and technologies.

Application Management Services

Our application maintenance and management services (AMS) support a broad range of enterprise IT systems from SAP and Oracle thereby reducing application environment costs and freeing up budgets for innovation and growth. Our full range of maintenance and support services begin from corrective maintenance, evolving to adaptive, preventive, and perfective maintenance. Whether your goal is to reduce costs, improve productivity and ROI or enhance competitiveness, we provide highly efficient management of your applications through our mature, standardized and highly reliable global processes and ITIL-based, SLA driven best practices.

Our approach goes much beyond merely managing and optimizing overgrown applications – we aim to optimize the technology, information architecture, people, and services of the client applications. We align our team structures based on our client’s business domain, technology roadmap, processes, and applications. Our technology expertise extends beyond SAP, PeopleSoft, Oracle, Microsoft technologies, Java, legacy systems, databases and several other platforms.
